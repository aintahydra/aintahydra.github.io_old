vmware-12.5-and-kernel-4.13

Issue

After updating the kernel to 4.13, VMware Workstation failed to start. After the update, VMware showed a popup window saying that it requires a module compilation, and then ended up with compilation error

Solution:

There is a patch:

  • https://communities.vmware.com/thread/568089
  • https://communities.vmware.com/servlet/JiveServlet/download/2688951-180357/VMware-Workstation-12.5.7-kernel4.13-atomic-inc.patch.zip
    Also there is another patch for a memory error:
  • https://superuser.com/questions/1258310/vware-workstation-14-and-new-hostif-c-on-kernel-4-13-for-fixing-a-memory-error
  • https://github.com/mkubecek/vmware-host-modules/blob/b50848c985f1a6c0a341187346d77f0119d0a835/vmmon-only/linux/hostif.c

    How-to

    Ref: https://iesodias.wordpress.com/2017/12/05/linux-host-kernel-4-13-rc1-breaks-vmnet-vmware-workstation-12/

Step 1

Use the patch at https://communities.vmware.com/servlet/JiveServlet/download/2688951-180357/VMware-Workstation-12.5.7-kernel4.13-atomic-inc.patch.zip

Step 2

Replace hostif.c with the downloaded version at https://github.com/mkubecek/vmware-host-modules/blob/b50848c985f1a6c0a341187346d77f0119d0a835/vmmon-only/linux/hostif.c
